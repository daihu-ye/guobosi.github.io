Biography

Received a Ph.D. degree in computer science from Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Science (CAS), Beijing, in Jun. 2020, advised by professor Shiguang Shan. I was also fortunate to be guided by Jiabei Zeng during my four year phd candidate career. Now i am an assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China. My research interests cover computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning. I especially focus on the face and facial expression anslysis.
